About GirlScript Jalandhar

GirlScript Foundation is a non-profit organization to support women in technology. Our main aim is to help beginners in Computer Science, IT and Security and to train them for facing the industry. But we not only teach, we also provide them information about various opportunities to speak, scholarships, fellowships etc. We are continuously working to bring 'Equality in Tech'​.
